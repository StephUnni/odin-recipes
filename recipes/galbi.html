Galbi Recipe

Description

This recipe is for galbi - probably the most expensive korean dish, but my favorite. Because yo girl got expensive taste.

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • minced garlic
  • sesame oil
  • la galbi shortribs

Steps

  1. mix soy sauce, sugar, minced garlic, and sesame oil
  2. coat all sides of the la galbi meat, let marinade for 1 hour refrigerated
  3. grill on high heat until slightly charred on outside
  4. enjoy!
